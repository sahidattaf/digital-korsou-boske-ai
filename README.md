# 🌳 Digital Kòrsou – Boske di AI

**AI ku Raís den Pueblo**

Digital Kòrsou – Boske di AI is a community-rooted artificial intelligence ecosystem designed for Curaçao.
It combines modern AI systems with local language (Papiamentu), culture, nature, and collective wisdom.

This is not just software.
This is a digital movement.

---

## 🌍 Vision
To empower the people of Curaçao with accessible, human-centered AI that respects culture, language, and community values.

---

## 🌳 What is the Boske di AI?
The Boske di AI (AI Forest) is a network of interconnected GPT agents.
Each agent is symbolized by a local tree or natural element and has a specific role:
- Education
- Jobs & income
- Culture & language
- Community support
- Sustainability
- Legal & ethics

All agents are connected through a central orchestrator.

---

## ✈️ Universal Arrival GPT
The entry point into the ecosystem.
Symbolically arrives from the sky and roots itself in the island.
Guides users into the right AI agent in Papiamentu or English.

---

## 🧠 Core Principles
- **Language first:** Papiamentu is a first-class language
- **Human before tech:** AI supports people, not replaces them
- **Community driven:** Built for schools, barrios, and local businesses
- **Open & modular:** Easy to extend and adapt
